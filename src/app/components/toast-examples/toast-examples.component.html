Key Features:

  • Severity Levels: success, info, warn, error
  • Positions: top-left, top-center, top-right, bottom-left, bottom-center, bottom-right
  • Customization: message, duration, severity, and positioning
  • Sticky Toasts: toasts that don't auto-dismiss (life: 0)
  • Multiple Toasts: show several toasts at once
  • Toast Management: clear specific or all toasts

Toast Properties:

  • severity: visual style and icon
  • summary: toast title
  • detail: toast message content
  • life: auto-dismiss time in milliseconds
  • key: unique identifier for positioning
  • sticky: prevents auto-dismiss
